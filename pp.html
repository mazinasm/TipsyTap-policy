Privacy Policy

TipsyTap does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Permissions used:
- SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW – required to display a floating icon on top of other apps.
- INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – required to load ads from Google AdMob.
- FOREGROUND_SERVICE – required to keep the service running in the background.

Third-party services:
TipsyTap uses Google AdMob for displaying ads. These services may collect anonymous usage data.

No user data is collected, stored, or shared by TipsyTap.
